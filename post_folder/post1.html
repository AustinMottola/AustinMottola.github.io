The 10 Laboratory Commandments

"Blessed is the scientists whose experiments are reproducible"

Posted by Active Agent on April 22, 2019

Rule 1

Thou shall neither eat nor drink whilst an experiment

Science cuts two ways, of course; its products can be used for both good and evil. But there's a general consensus on this rule

Rule 2

Thou shall not covet thy neighbour's result

Those who break this rule shall be called the sons of villanious decepticons

Rule 3

Honour the Lab assistant you met in the lab

Those who break this rule have been reported to toil in the pit of abysmal confusion, for to unlock Eureka you must see beyond the beaker

The dreams of yesterday are the hopes of today and the reality of tomorrow. Science has not yet mastered prophecy. We predict too much for the next year and yet far too little for the next ten.

Rule 4

If you emptied IT refill IT

The chinese have a saying, those who think highly of their colleagues will never face the shame to sit alone at lunch

Rule 5

One for all all for one

The 3 masked men used this phrase for solidarity, it's also relevant for the Laboratory, we need each other, simple

Rule 6

Never leave an uncleaned work bench and never use one

for cleanliness is next to godliness

Rule 7

If you did not see it do not claim it

for the 7th ring of hell is a hot and dreadful place for liars

Rule 8

Writing before experimenting

To speak is human to write is divine especially if done in a legible and concise manner
